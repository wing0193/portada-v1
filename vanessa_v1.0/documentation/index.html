Background: Video

index-video.html

How to insert your video?

1. Go to the video folder

 

2. There are 2 video formats: mp4 and webm. Convert your videos in this formats.

The easiest way to do it online here: Online video converter

3. Insert your poster.jpg.

The poster will be displayed on devices in of unsupported video and will be displayed before video loaded

Change map

How to change the map?

Go to the js folder and open gmap.js

1. Change coordinates: latitude and longitude
Watch video "how to get google maps latitude longitude". Video

2. If needs, change zoom of the map

3. Change address details

4. Change title

Dont forget to change the address in HTML code too!
